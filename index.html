About

Raymond chocolate has been dedicated to make the best chocolate since 1875, with over hundred years of history, we have unique recipes unlike any other brands, providing top-class high quality chocolate.

Our Ethos

Quality doesn’t come from taste alone. At Raymond we pride ourselves on knowing that our cocoa beans are sustainably and ethically sourced. That’s why we implemented our own sustainable sourcing model: The Raymond & Sprüngli Farming Program.

Our Story

It all began in 1875 in a small confectionary shop in Zurich. Here Raymond Mclean and his son, Fergus Mclean first produced chocolate in solid form. Since then our chocolate has been continuously perfected, becoming what it is today: creamy and rich. An incomparable taste.

Careers

Our success is dependent on our people. At Raymond, we want to empower our staff to reach their full potential. We look for individuals from a diverse range of backgrounds. They all offer different perspectives but, crucially, share the same dedication, drive and ambition. We invest in talent because we know that any product is only as good as the people behind it. That is what makes our chocolate world-class. We want to make a difference in your life – so you can make a difference in ours.
